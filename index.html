About me

I am currently a research assistant in the IxD Lab of the School of Design at Shanghai Jiao Tong University at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a Bachelor of Science degree Major in Mathematical Physics and Minor in Biophysics in 2021, and I have spent 2 years in the healthcare industry at Shanghai.

My research interests include bioinformatics, biomedical science, and cancer biology. Specifically, I am devoted to the research of utilizing AI techniques to improve biomedical research.
